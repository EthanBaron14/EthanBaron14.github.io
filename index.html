About Me

Hi!

My name is Ethan and I am a third-year student pursuing a Bachelors degree in Computer Science and Finance.

I attend school during the academic year at The George Washington University in Washington, DC. Accordingly, I am pursuing coursework in the George Washington University School of Business, School of Engineering and Applied Science, and University Honors Program.

When I'm not at school, I enjoy visiting my family in Greenvale, NY. Only about 40 minutes from New York City, I am fortunate enough to live near two of the best cities in the United States.

I am actively involved extracurricularly in both disciplines that I am studying. Starting in Fall 2019, I accepted a position where I have since served as a member of course instructional staff in the Computer Science department. The same semester, I rushed a professional business fraternity and have had the privilege of serving on its executive committee ever since.

When I have some time off, I enjoy going out to eat with my friends, visiting museums to learn more about the world, and attending electronic music concerts.
